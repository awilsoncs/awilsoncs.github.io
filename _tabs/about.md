Welcome to my blog! I am an independent game developer and enthusiast passionate about sharing my experiences and perspectives. This blog will share my thoughts, insights, and musings on leadership, game development, and other industry aspects, drawing on my own experiences and observations to provide a unique and personal perspective.

I have one major game release and several business and minor game projects to my name. Through these years in games and other software pursuits, I have met and worked with many talented people and learned from their experiences and insights.

On this blog, you will find various articles and posts that cover topics related to game development's technical, business, and even philosophical sides. I will share my own experiences and observations and provide tips and advice based on what I have learned and what has worked for me. I will also offer my opinions and thoughts on the latest trends and developments in the gaming industry. I will share my insights and perspectives on game developers' challenges and opportunities.

My blog will be a valuable resource for game developers, team leads, and other gaming enthusiasts looking to learn more about the industry and stay informed on the latest trends and developments. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy my blog!
